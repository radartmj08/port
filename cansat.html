Thailand CANSAT-ROCKET Competition 2024

Self-developed CANSAT delivery with integrated tracking system for real-time monitoring of shipment status and location.

Project Overview

The boom in online sales has made shipping a critical factor. Air freight is widely used for its convenience and speed, but many goods are fragile. To ensure product safety and prevent damage, a system needs to be developed. This team proposes a damage prevention system, using chicken and quail eggs as proxies for fragile goods, that will monitor and alert the package's location and critical flight data (temperature, air pressure, light intensity) via a user-friendly mobile application.

Mission Objectives

  • 1

    To build a CANSAT capable of collecting data on its temperature, air pressure, light intensity, location, and acceleration.

  • 2

    To develop a shock-protection system for the chicken and quail eggs inside the CANSAT.

  • 3

    To create an application that displays the CANSAT's weather conditions and location.
