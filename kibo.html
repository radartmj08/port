KiboCUBE

KiboCUBE is a university‑level program by UNOOSA and JAXA that offers opportunities to deploy 1U CubeSats from the ISS Kibo module. Teams compete via proposal, design review, integration, and deployment phases to realize an end‑to‑end satellite mission.

Competition Overview

KiboCUBE (UNOOSA/JAXA) grants selected university teams access to space by enabling deployment of a 1U CubeSat from the ISS Kibo module. The program emphasizes mission feasibility, safety, and education while requiring a complete space mission lifecycle: concept, design, integration, operations, and data return.

Mission Objectives

  • 1
    To observe floods for flood management and agricultural protection.
  • 2
    To help preserve farmland and agricultural products, elevate agricultural standards, and support pollution‑management policy.
  • 3
    To create passion and interest in the space technology field and build understanding of satellite usage and development.
