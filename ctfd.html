Thailand Cyber Talent 2025

A national cybersecurity competition (CTF) covering network security, web exploitation, cryptography, forensics, and reverse engineering across qualifier and final rounds.

Competition Overview

Thailand Cyber Talent (TCT) is a national-level Capture The Flag (CTF) competition that evaluates practical cybersecurity skills through hands‑on challenges in networking, web, crypto, forensics, reverse engineering, and system exploitation. The format includes an online qualification round followed by onsite finals.

What I Learned

  • 2
    Hands‑on tooling: network analysis, web testing, cryptography, and binary reversing
  • 3
    Team collaboration, write‑ups, and time‑boxed triage to maximize points
