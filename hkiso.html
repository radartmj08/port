Competition Overview

HKISO (Hong Kong International Science Olympiad) is a science competition that assesses scientific thinking, analysis, and real‑world application across Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Physical Science.

What I Learned

  • 1
    Scientific thinking: forming hypotheses and designing experiments
  • 2
    Solving physics and chemistry problems using fundamentals and dimensional analysis
  • 3
    Communicating results clearly with sound reasoning
