TIMO — Thailand International Mathematical Olympiad

An international mathematics competition focused on deep problem solving, logical reasoning, and creativity across Algebra, Number Theory, Geometry, and Combinatorics.

Competition Overview

TIMO is a mathematics competition that assesses analytical and creative problem solving across qualifying and final rounds, covering core Olympiad topics.

What I Learned

  • 1
    Mathematical problem solving and rigorous proof techniques
  • 2
    Applying Algebra, Number Theory, Geometry, and Combinatorics
  • 3
    Time management and contest strategy

Exam Topics

Algebra & Number Theory
  • • Equations/inequalities, functions, polynomials
  • • Divisibility, residues, factorization
  • • Sequences/series and advanced counting
  • • Variable transformations/invariants
Geometry & Combinatorics
  • • Synthetic/coordinate geometry, angle and area theorems
  • • Similarity/symmetry, notable points (centroid, incenter, etc.)
  • • Addition–multiplication principles, Pigeonhole, basic graphs
  • • Advanced counting and permutations
Awards Received
  • • Qualified in Heat Round
  • • Advanced to Final Round
  • • Gold Award in Heat Round
